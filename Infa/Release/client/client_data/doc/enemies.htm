Examples

Enemies.HealthMin - health level of the weakest known enemy.

Enemies.HealthMax - health level of the strongest known enemy.

Enemies.HealthAvg - average health level of the known enemies.

Enemies.DistanceMin - minimal distance toward the known enemy.

Enemies.DistanceMax- maximum distance toward the known enemy.

Enemies.DistanceAvg- average distance toward the known enemies.

Enemies.TimeSeeMin - time since you have last seen any enemy.

Enemies.TimeSeeMax- time since you have last seen the enemy you've met most long ago.

Enemies.TimeSeeAvg- average time since you have last seen your enemies.

Enemies.Number - the number of known enemies.
