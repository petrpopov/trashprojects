Examples

Waypoints.MinDist - distance toward the closest waypoint.

Waypoints.MaxDist - distance toward the farthest waypoint.

Waypoints.AvgDist - average distance toward the known waypoints.

Waypoints.MinNewDist - minimum distance between two known waypoints.

Waypoints.MaxNewDist - maximum distance between two known waypoints.

Waypoints.AvgNewDist - average distance between known waypoints.

Waypoints.Number - the number of known waypoints.
