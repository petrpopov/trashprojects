When setting conditions you may use logical operators "and", "or", "not" and nested bracket expressions. You may also use the comparison operators <,> and =. Here are the functions that may be applied to in conditions:

  • CanGoForward
  • CanGoLeft
  • CanGoRight
  • CanGoBack
  • IsAvailablePoint (x, y)
  • FinishIsKnown
  • IsDefinedWaypoint
  • WaypointExists (line1, line2)

It is also possible to use expressions of comparing different properties (your robot's, enemies' robots', known map's) between each other and with integer numbers.

Here is an example of a correct condition:

if ( not (CanGoForward or CanGoBack) and (Enemies.Number > 0) and (My.Health < Target.Health) ) { actions }
